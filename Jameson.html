    Jameson Irish Whiskey

    Price: $15.99

    • Alcohol degree: From 35 ° to 45 °
    • Origin: Cork (originally Dublin), Ireland
    • Manufacturer: Irish Distillers (Pernod Ricard)
    • Whiskey type: Bourbon
    • Volume: 700 ml.

    Jameson is produced from a blend of grain whiskey and single pot still whiskey, which uses a mixture of malted and unmalted or "green" Irish barley, all sourced from within a fifty-mile radius around the distillery in Cork. The barley is dried in a closed kiln fired by natural gas (formerly anthracite coal). This is in contrast to the traditional method used in some Scotch whisky distilleries, which fire the kiln with peat, adding a distinctive peat flavour.n 2018 Jameson 18-Year-Old Bow Street won Best Irish Blended Whiskey RRP of €60/$72 or more at the Irish Whiskey Awards.
